## What is This System?

The Disbursement Voucher Tracker digitizes and automates the entire process of creating, reviewing, approving, and tracking disbursement vouchers. Instead of paper documents moving between departments, everything happens electronically with real-time tracking, automated notifications, and complete audit trails.
