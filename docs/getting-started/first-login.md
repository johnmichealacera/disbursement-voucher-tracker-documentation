# First-Time Login

Welcome! This guide will help you access the Disbursement Voucher Tracker for the first time and get familiar with the interface.

## Accessing the Application

### Step 1: Open Your Web Browser

Open your preferred web browser. We recommend using **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** for the best experience.

### Step 2: Navigate to the Application URL

Your system administrator will provide you with the web address (URL) for the Disbursement Voucher Tracker. It will typically look like:
- `https://voucher-tracker.yourmunicipality.gov.ph`
- Or a similar web address

Type this address into your browser's address bar and press **Enter**.

### Step 3: The Login Page

You'll see the login page with:
- **Username** field
- **Password** field
- **Login** button

:::info Security Note
The login page uses secure encryption. Look for the padlock icon (ðŸ”’) in your browser's address bar to confirm the connection is secure.
:::

### Step 4: Enter Your Credentials

1. Click in the **Username** field
2. Type your username (provided by your administrator)
3. Click in the **Password** field
4. Type your password (provided by your administrator)

:::warning Password Security
- Passwords are case-sensitive (capital and lowercase letters matter)
- Make sure Caps Lock is not enabled
- If you can't see what you're typing, that's normalâ€”passwords are hidden for security
:::

### Step 5: Click Login

Click the **Login** button to access the system.

### Step 6: First Login Success

If your credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to your **Dashboard**. Congratulationsâ€”you're in!

:::danger Login Issues
If you see an error message:
- **"Invalid username or password"**: Double-check your credentials, ensure Caps Lock is off
- **"Account is inactive"**: Contact your administratorâ€”your account may need activation
- **"Session expired"**: Try logging in again
- **Page won't load**: Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page
:::

## Understanding Your User Role

After logging in, the system recognizes your **user role**. Your role determines:
- What menu options you see
- What actions you can perform
- Which vouchers you can view
- What notifications you receive

### Common Roles

- **REQUESTER**: You create disbursement vouchers
- **GSO**: You create procurement-related vouchers
- **HR**: You create HR-related vouchers
- **SECRETARY**: You review and route vouchers
- **MAYOR**: You approve vouchers
- **BAC**: You review procurement vouchers
- **BUDGET**: You verify budget allocation
- **ACCOUNTING**: You validate financial information
- **TREASURY**: You process payments
- **ADMIN**: You manage users and system settings

:::tip Role Information
Your role is displayed in your profile or dashboard. If you're unsure about your role or permissions, contact your system administrator.
:::

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your **Dashboard**â€”your home page in the system. The dashboard shows:

### Key Sections

1. **Welcome Message**: Personalized greeting with your name
2. **Quick Statistics**: Overview of vouchers relevant to you
3. **Pending Actions**: Items requiring your attention
4. **Recent Activity**: Latest updates on vouchers you're involved with
5. **Status Summary**: Count of vouchers by status

### What You'll See (Role-Specific)

#### For Requesters, GSO, and HR Users
- Number of vouchers you've created
- Status of your vouchers (Draft, Pending, Approved, etc.)
- Recent voucher activity

#### For Reviewers (Secretary, Mayor, BAC, Budget, Accounting)
- Number of vouchers pending your review
- Vouchers awaiting your action
- Recently reviewed vouchers

#### For Treasury Users
- Vouchers ready for check issuance
- Vouchers available for release
- Recently released vouchers

#### For Admin Users
- System-wide statistics
- User management overview
- Directory management status

## Navigation Menu Guide

The **navigation menu** is typically located at the top of the page or in a sidebar. It contains links to different sections of the system.

### Common Menu Items

- **Dashboard**: Return to your home page
- **Disbursements**: View and manage vouchers
- **Create Request**: Create a new voucher (for Requesters, GSO, HR)
- **My Vouchers**: View vouchers you created (for Requesters, GSO, HR)
- **Pending Reviews**: Vouchers requiring your review (for Reviewers)
- **User Management**: Manage users (Admin only)
- **Directory Management**: Manage payees, tags, items (Admin only)
- **System Settings**: Configure system settings (Admin only)

:::info Menu Visibility
The menu items you see depend on your role. Don't worry if you don't see certain optionsâ€”that's normal based on your permissions.
:::

## Profile Management Basics

### Accessing Your Profile

Look for your name or a profile icon in the top-right corner of the page. Click it to access:
- Your profile information
- Account settings
- Logout option

### Important Profile Information

- **Your Name**: Displayed throughout the system
- **Your Role**: Determines your permissions
- **Your Department**: Important for Requesters (determines which vouchers you can create)
- **Email**: Used for notifications (if configured)

### Logging Out

When you're done using the system:
1. Click your profile/name in the top-right
2. Click **Logout** or **Sign Out**
3. You'll be returned to the login page

:::warning Security Best Practice
Always log out when you're finished, especially on shared computers. This protects your account and the system.
:::
